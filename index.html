Chong Bao

I am a third-year Ph.D. student at the State Key Lab of CAD&CG, Zhejiang University, advised by Prof. Guofeng Zhang and Prof. Zhaopeng Cui.

My research interests are in 3D computer vision and AR/VR, specifically neural rendering, structure from motion, and SLAM.
